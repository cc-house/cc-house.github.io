Bournemouth Chinese Christian Fellowship

Demonstrating God's love together in Bournemouth. We do this by celebrating the new life He has given us as we believe and follow Jesus Christ.

We are a Fellowship

We are not a building, we are a body of believers with Jesus the head.

Prayer Request

Please pray for us as we develop this new fellowship.
  • Particularly that we effectively equip Chinese students to take the Gospel of Jesus Christ back to China.
  • That we are supported by more native Chinese speaking Christian leaders.
  • We are guided by the Holy Spirit to grow this fellowship in a God honouring way.
